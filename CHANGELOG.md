### Added
- Student Inquiries feature:

  - Dashboard page to view/manage inquiries with a polished Tailwind table UI.
  - Email notifications to Staff Cohort 2025 on new inquiry.
  - Email to student when inquiry is marked resolved/completed.
- Reusable Navbar and Footer components applied across public pages.
- Documentation: docs/ACHIEVEMENT_Student_Inquiries.md, includes WhatsApp-ready announcement text.
- README updated with a "What's New" section referencing the achievement.
- Case Guidelines document for assessment (public/docs/CASE_GUIDELINES.html) and a download endpoint at /download/case-guidelines.
