MUBS Internship Portal – Case Document Version 1.0

Date: 07 Nov 2025

a) Case Description & Purpose (4 Marks)

Case: Makerere University Business School (MUBS) requires a streamlined way for students to submit internship placement information and for staff to manage, track, and respond to related inquiries.

Organization: MUBS – Internship Coordination Office (ICO).

Purpose of the Website:

  • Provide a centralized, secure portal for students to submit internship details (organization, contacts, course, notes).
  • Enable administrators to review, approve, and manage student submissions and inquiries efficiently.
  • Improve communication through automated email notifications (e.g., confirmation to students, alerts to staff cohort 2025, resolution updates).
  • Offer up‑to‑date announcements and deadlines to keep students informed.

b) Website Structure (4 Marks)

  • Home (/or /home)
    • Hero banner with welcome message and “Get Started”.
    • How It Works (4 steps: submit, verify, track, approve).
    • Student Internship Submission Form (name, reg. number, course, organization, contacts, email, notes).
    • Announcements (deadlines, new partners).
    • FAQ and testimonials.
  • About (/about)
    • Purpose and objectives of the portal.
    • Benefits (time saving, real‑time updates, transparency).
    • Team/Support contact details.
  • Contact (/contact)
    • Contact information (email, phone, office, hours).
    • Student Inquiry form (full name, email, reg. number, subject, message) with success banner on submit.
    • Location map (embedded).
  • Dashboard (/dashboard, authenticated)
    • Admin view with navigation and analytics.
    • Student Inquiries page (/inquiries): professional data table listing inquiries with status badges, submitted date, and actions.
    • Actions include: mark as done (updates status to completed/resolved and emails the student) and delete.

c) Design & Features (2 Marks)

Look & Feel: Clean, modern, and responsive interface using Tailwind CSS. Color palette centers on MUBS‑appropriate blues for trust and clarity, light neutrals for backgrounds, and accessible contrast for text and buttons.

Layout & Components

  • Reusable Navbar (brand: “MUBS INTERNSHIP PORTAL”) with Home, About, Contact, and auth actions (Login/Register or Dashboard/Logout).
  • Reusable Footer with contact and social links.
  • Cards, badges, and tables styled for clarity and professionalism.

Key Features

  • Student submission form with validation and success messages.
  • Inquiry workflow: submissions default to Pending; staff cohort 2025 notified via email; resolution triggers a student email.
  • Dashboard management: status updates, deletion, and pagination.
  • Accessibility: focus states, semantic sections, and responsive design.

Concept kept clear and realistic; scope aligns with actual implemented functionality in the current system.
